Shares of German companies on the Russian stock market.

Shares of German companies on the Russian stock market.

Hello, dear readers.

With the appearance of an increasing number of investors in our country, in the person of ordinary individuals, our stock market began to actively develop.

Brokers began to reduce commissions and improve their applications in order to attract as many customers as possible.

Banks are increasingly issuing analogous foreign ETFs in the form of exchange-traded funds or BiFs, in order to attract as many clients as possible.

the exchange has launched evening trading and will soon introduce the sale of large American companies. Of course, to attract both as many clients as possible.

The St. Petersburg stock exchange was also involved.

Public joint stock company "Saint Petersburg stock exchange" was founded in 1997. But initially, it was used for trading futures and options.

Saint Petersburg launched trading in foreign securities by Department stores only in November 2014 years. And at the moment, more than 1000 shares of foreign companies are traded here.

This week, SPb announced that the list of securities that will be admitted to trading will be updated.

This list included 52 shares of German companies, such as

Adidas

Deutsche Bank

HUGO BOSS

Puma

Siemens

Deutsche Lufthansa

Volkswagen

and others.

The launch of trading of these securities will take place in August.

I think this is great news. Since investors will have a chance to invest in one of the most developed economies in Europe.

For for some, this will be a great opportunity to diversify their portfolio by country. And maybe even by currency.

Although there is no information yet in which currency these securities will be traded: in dollars or euros. But personally I'm already getting started look out for yourself stocks from this list and start to accumulate euros.

Do you think that German stocks will be in demand here? And would you include them in your portfolio?

To everyone successful investment!
