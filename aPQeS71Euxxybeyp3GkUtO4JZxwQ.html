Should I start investing with 10,000 rubles or accumulate a larger amount?

Should I start investing with 10,000 rubles or accumulate a larger amount?

Good afternoon, everyone.

I often find myself thinking on Zen that there is nothing to do on the stock exchange without 50-100 thousand rubles.

If you listen to independent financial consultants, they generally talk, as for not having a free $ 10,000, it is better to stay where you are and spend this money on your education, health or vacation.

I don't agree with this point of view.

No, I am not a financial adviser of any kind, and I do not intend to become one, but I am sure that everyone has the right to achieve this goal financial freedom.

Yes, those who have a free $ 10,000 will achieve financial independence faster than those who have only 10,000 rubles.

But this does not mean that having only 10 thousand rubles is not necessary to get involved in the game called "stock market".

Starting from 10,000 dollars more chances to create from the very beginning start a balanced portfolio by buying stocks from different industries and possibly countries.

A beginner with 10,000 rubles will need more time to create the same balanced portfolio. But that doesn't mean he doesn't have a chance.

Yes, the result will also be different. And this needs to be understood and understood.

Of course, if there is an opportunity to accumulate a larger amount, it is better to start with it.

But let's be honest. Not everyone can save money when they are lying around and "itching to spend them."

And if it were so easy to save up, then everyone would have at least a million rubles in their stash. Well, if there is no such money, then it does not work to save. There are always reasons to spend money.

Saving in investments is easier - seeing how your money works, there is no longer a thought spend it on a new refrigerator or winter "shoes" for the car.

And if there are problems that require financial intervention, then the brain begins to work and look for options to raise money, without withdrawing money from a brokerage account.

And the most important thing in investing is not how much money you want to invest. start amounts, and regularity.
