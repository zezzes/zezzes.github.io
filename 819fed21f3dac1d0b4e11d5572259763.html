The first gold bullion Fund in Russia.

The first gold bullion Fund in Russia.

Good day to you all.

Well, our stock market continues to actively develop, offering more and more assets to retail investors.

And just a few days ago, there was news that the first exchange-traded Fund for physical assets will be launched in Russia gold. What does it mean?

In Western exchange-traded funds or ETFs for gold, investors ' money buys gold bars that are stored in banks. That is, when investors buy these funds, they buy physical gold.

What about Russia? Yes, we have them too there are several different exchange-traded funds or gold ETFs, but they use investors ' money to buy shares in Western gold ETFs. Or they just track gold prices at all, without buying any ETFs.

And now, on November 2,VTB Capital Investmentsmanagement company starts updating itsVTB-Fund Gold. Stockexchange ". VTBG Ticker.

During the month, the management company plans to gradually exit the securities of the SPDR Gold Shares ETF, replacing them with physical gold bars, which will be stored in VTB Bank.

Let me remind you that this exchange-traded Fund from VTB Capital management company was launched in June of this year. But until today, it was a paper Fund, because it used investors ' money to buy Western ETFs.

Now its strategy is changing and the first exchange-traded Fund for physical gold appears in Russia.

Commissions the funds will not exceed 0.66% per annum, and the cost of the Fund will not change. Now its price is somewhere around 1 ruble.

What does this mean for me?

In principle, I do not bet much on gold now, since it has already increased in price. But the appearance of such a Fund in our country - for me-means one thing-managers companies are still starting to think in the right direction, offering us, private investors, something new. What is available in other countries, but not yet in Russia. And not offering the same Fund, only from another CC.
