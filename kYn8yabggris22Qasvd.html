The budget portfolio. Week 49. "Money must work."

The budget portfolio. Week 49. "Money must work."

Hello, dear readers! I welcome you to my blog, where I talk about investments, conduct financial experiments and show you all this.

Today is Sunday, July 5, 2020. And as always I I'm posting a report on what I bought in my budget portfolio this week.

Monday started for the budget portfolio with the purchase of the dollar.

The dollar this week exceeded the mark of 70 rubles per piece, and therefore I switch back to buying only$ 1 in the portfolio instead of two, because I try to reduce purchases currencies when it grows.

On Tuesday, June 30, I took into my portfolio a bond of Udmurtia with a maturity in October of this year.

In October, I will have a lot of repayments, there will be a whole thousand-ruble obliga of repayments there.

On Wednesday, I bought 100 rubles in Chinese yuan. It turned out less than usual. Therefore, I think the purchase of yuan should also be reduced for the time being, while the ruble sausages.

In Thursday-one exchange-traded Fund of the Moscow Exchange Index. The index has recently been actively trading in a sideways direction, it does not grow much, but it does not fall. I am concerned about this, because for me it means that investors do not really believe in good growth, and therefore do not rush to invest their money in the market.

On Friday, I again chose which stock to buy. The majority of shares in this portfolio are still held in the green zone amid the expectation of dividends. Therefore, I choose only from those who have not decided on divas or do not yet pay.

So I took 1 share of Aeroflot, continuing to average my position.

On Saturday, she added Eurobonds to the mutual Fund. Also now keeps in the black because of the weakening of the ruble. I understand that such investments as Eurobonds are currently at their peak and we should abstract about them for now.

Well, today I took bitcoin for 100 rubles. Cryptocurrency has recently been dragging around somewhere in the sideways-minus, it begins to lose my interest in her. In principle, I have never had any interest in the crypt, but before, at least the price jumped, and now it is slowly sliding down.

As a result, this week has shown that it is time to reconsider again the strategy of this portfolio. Since the March fall, I have often revised it, but I understand that investing in a crisis and in a non-crisis should be different. Although some people think the opposite, the strategy should be the same, regardless of the market situation.

In short, I am now considering what to do next. If there's any thoughts on this subject, please write in the comments.

Well, what about my funny "penny briefcase"? It makes me happy. It is completely in the black. Plus, of course, relative, because the invested amount is less than 100 rubles.

Such it was a week for my experimental portfolios.

How did you remember this week? Write in the comments.

All successful investments!
